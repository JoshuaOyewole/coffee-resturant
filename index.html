Welcome to Orisfina Coffee Bar

Orisfina Coffee Bar is the vision of Joshua Oyewole, who on discovering the coffee culture of Nigeria saw an opportunity to enrich the Edo landscape. Working with Orisfina group of companies, the drinks at Orisfina bar are crafted with the goal always to showcase the intrinsic character of individual coffees and served with casual familiarity. Paired with locally homemade pastries, the cafe offers everything needed for a quick stop on the way to work or a catch-up with an old friend.
